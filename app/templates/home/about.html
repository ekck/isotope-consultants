ISOTOPE CONSULTANCY LIMITED is committed to setting the highest standards for service delivery and performance in environmental impact assessments and audits as well as consultancy services on environmental conservation and management.

The following outline the basic services provided by the Firm:

  • Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA)
  • Environmental Management Plan (EMP)
  • Environmental Audit (EA)
  • Environmental Policy and Legislative Compliance Application

The Firm of Experts, given their knowledge in the existing environmental policies, laws and regulations will produce high quality, time and more cost-effective EIA and EA reports.

About Us

  • REGISTRATION

    The company is duly registered under the Companies Act 2015 of the Laws of Kenya and incorporated here in Kenya as a Private Limited Liability Company. We are also registered with the Ministry of Finance (Kenya Revenue Authority).

  • Setting Project Goals

    The Firm of Experts will always thoroughly familiarize themselves with the project/ activity complexities through site visits and site meetings with the stakeholders and any other parties involved in projects to ensure public participation. These meetings are useful in reviewing projects/ activities and lines of authority as well as establishing project procedures and rules, while identifying possible impacts a particular project/ activity might have on the environment. Thereafter, a work plan will be developed to facilitate easy co-ordination of environmental assessments and/or audits which will guide in developing an EMP for project implementation or otherwise according to the verdict that will be given by National Environment Management Authority (NEMA).

  • Information and Documentation

    ISOTOPE CONSULTANCY LIMITED believes in maintaining records to facilitate easy documentation and retrieval of any information required at project/ activity site and office. It includes reports for project/ activity site meetings which focus on action items for each of the participants. The action items guide the entire process of ESIA and/or EA and can be tracked and managed by the Experts to ensure they are implemented to completion during project/activity process. The final EIA and/or EA report will be forwarded to NEMA complete with EMP for every project/activity verified and signed by all Experts who participated in the exercise.

  • Establishment and Monitoring Quality

    ISOTOPE CONSULTANTS LIMITED is committed to timely delivery of services and any other item that require its attention as required and when needed. Similarly, the Firm of Experts is responsible for co-ordination of sampling and testing of all required materials as set out in the environmental laws, policies, regulations and standard specification requirements. During every operation, Firm of Experts will check standard procedures and observe the entire process to meet the stipulated environmental standards such as ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management systems.

  • Commitment to Health, Safety and the Environment

    Activities of ISOTOPE CONSULTANCY LIMITED are executed in accordance with the guidelines provided by the Government and the National Environment Management Authority (NEMA) as well as our comprehensive company policies and procedures and other relevant legislations. The Firm implements the occupational health and safety requirements and delegate obligations to the Experts to ensure compliance at the projects/activity site(s).
